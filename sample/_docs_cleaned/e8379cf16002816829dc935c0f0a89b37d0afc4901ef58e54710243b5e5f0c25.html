Details Logo  Go Add to Favorites Japanese: ㈜아이코닉스 Established: Sep 25, 2001 Member Favorites: 0 Iconix Entertainment is a South Korean production company founded by Choi Jong-il on September 25, 2001. It is located in Seongnam-si, Gyeonggi-do, Seoul, South Korea. It is the successor of Geumgang Planning's animation team. The company is best known for producing CGI-animated shows such as Pororo the Little Penguin, Tayo the Little Bus and many more. Iconix also produced anime series as well such as Guardian Fairy Michel, Tai Chi Chasers and Flowering Heart.
