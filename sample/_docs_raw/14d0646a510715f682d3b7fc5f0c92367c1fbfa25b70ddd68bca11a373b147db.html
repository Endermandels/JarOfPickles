Founded in 2019, Shanghai Fantum Animation is committed to creating high-quality 2D animation and participating in the production of many excellent animation works. Most of the main creative cores are animation workers who have returned from Japan, and they have very rich experience in 2D animation production. Based on the whole process of two-dimensional animation production, the company undertakes the production of fan dramas, short films, advertising animations, game PV promotional animations, etc. The picture style is mainly based on two-dimensional animation and can incorporate a variety of creative elements to enrich the picture.
