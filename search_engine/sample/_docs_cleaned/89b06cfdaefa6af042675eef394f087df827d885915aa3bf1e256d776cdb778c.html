The two of its three founders, Wang Bin and Zhou Bin, are 2009 and 2010 graduates of Tsinghua University respectively. Since it was founded in 2014, the company has grown from a small studio with only three founders to an outstanding start-up of up to 50 people with its core members all from Tsinghua University. Now it focuses on creating original 2D animation while developing animation derivatives gallery and incubating multiple original animations.
