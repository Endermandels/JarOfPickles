Details Logo  Go Add to Favorites Japanese: 서울무비 Established: Jan, 1990 Dissolved: 2008 Member Favorites: 0 Seoul Movie was a South Korean animation studio that was founded in January 1990 and established on January 10, 1994. The company was known for producing [i]Dooly the Little Dinosaur: Ice Star Adventure[/i] movie. They would later go on to produce [i]Yorang-a Yorang-a[/i], [i]The Marshmallow Times[/i] and many more. Their final show was [i]Petit Petit Muse[/i]. After the show cancelled Seoul Movie shut down in 2008.
